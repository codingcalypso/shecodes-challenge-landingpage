Activities to do in Oklahoma

Downtown Oklahoma City is a major attraction for visitors. OKC offers a ton of nice restaurants, museums, shopping, and outodor activities such as hiking, climing, and watersports. Oklahoma boasts around 235 days of sunshine throughout the year so there is plenty of nice weather to enjoy through the year.

Sports is also a huge attraction in Oklahoma. We have the Oklahoma City Thunder, an NBA team, as well as the college football favorites: The University of Oklahoma Sooners (OU), and the Oklahoma State Cowboys (OSU).
